English as a Second Language Learning

  • Worldwide use of English
  • Language learners want to learn English

Metalinguistic Awareness

  • The ability to reflect upon language
    • To think about its rules, structures, and latent meaning
  • Essential skill acquired by language learners
  • Syntatic metalinguistic awareness
    • Analyse whether a sentence is grammatical or not

Language Transfer

  • Common language learning strategy
    • Often automatic
  • Application of first language rules in second language utterances
    • What happens when the rules in the two languages do not match?

Negative Language Transfer

  • Learners are not always aware that they are transferring from their first languages
  • The mismatch between language rules causes errors
  • Example: Russian native speakers and English articles
[The] most common way to earn money is babysitting.
